Our Story

Empowering Innovation in Electrical Distribution Systems

Founded in 2018, Initiative Engineering LLC specializes in electrical distribution systems. We offer design and engineering services, along with low-volume harness manufacturing for early development phases. Recognizing that electrical harnessing often receives insufficient attention during initial program stages, we aim to bridge this gap. Whether you're an OEM or a Tier 1/2 supplier, our network of experienced harness designers and engineers ensures your projects progress seamlessly.

We support OEMs and Tier 1/2 suppliers by providing experienced harness designers and engineers, ensuring seamless project progression. Our manufacturing solutions cater to early proof-of-concept needs and scale with partners for higher, pre-production volumes, aligning with your production sourcing strategies.
